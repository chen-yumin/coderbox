What is Floating Point Converter?

IEEE Standard 754 floating point is the most common representation today for real numbers on computers, including Intel-based PC's, Macintoshes, and most Unix platforms.

This loating Point Converter helps you convert single (32-bit) or double (64-bit) precision floating-point values to its IEEE Standard 754 floating point in binary and hexdecimal representation.
